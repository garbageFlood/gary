Computing Support Service


The computing support service comprises two full time tutors: one for full-time programmes and another dedicated to part-time programmes. In addition, the service has a panel of part-time tutors that can provide tuition in any of the computing modules on offer in the School of Computing at NCI.

SWAT Workshops


In semester one the Computing Support Service runs a series of workshops for first year students focussing on the skills needed when settling into a new college course. These include topics such as, touch typing, referencing, research etc. These sessions are not mandatory but attendance is strongly recommended.

One to One & Small Group Tutorials


The Computing Support Tutor is available to work with students on a one to one or small group basis. Students that find that they are falling behind in a module or that are finding a particular topic difficult to understand should contact the Support Tutor for an appointment and get caught up before it becomes a problem. To make an appointment email computingsupport@ncirl.ie with a suggested time that you are free to meet.

Whole Class Tutorials


If a class are finding a particular module or topic difficult and need an additional tutorial, the lecturer or class rep may contact the Support Tutor who will then schedule an extra tutorial for the whole class.

Drop In Workshops


The Computing Support Service runs weekly support workshops for first year students in Computer Programming. These sessions are scheduled in your timetable from week 3 or 4 onwards. If you have a small question or query you can always just pop into the session and ask or you might simply come along and use the time to work on additional programmes or project work with the support of the tutor. No appointment needed! Where additional tutorials on particular topics are requested, the workshops can also be used as an additional tutorial session instead.

Moodle


The Computing Support Service also has a Moodle page where you can find a schedule of extra classes and Drop in Workshop hours as well as past exam papers and revision material for some modules. Enrol yourself on the Computing Support Moodle page by following this link: http://moodle.ncirl.ie/course/view.php?id=1199

Exam Revision


Exam revision sessions will be held in the run up to each exam session. These sessions will take you through past or sample exam questions and solutions in preparation for the exams.
